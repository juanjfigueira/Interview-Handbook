
# Chapter 1 - Professional Story

> **Goal**
>
> Learn how to introduce yourself naturally, confidently, and professionally during HR and technical interviews.

---

# Why This Chapter Matters

Almost every interview starts with one of these questions:

- Tell me about yourself.
- Walk me through your resume.
- Can you introduce yourself?

Your answer sets the tone for the rest of the interview.

A strong introduction creates confidence.

A weak introduction forces the interviewer to spend the next thirty minutes trying to understand your profile.

This chapter is designed to help you communicate your professional story naturally instead of memorizing scripts.

---

# Communication Principles

During interviews:

✔ Tell a story.

✔ Focus on impact.

✔ Explain business value.

✔ Mention technologies only when relevant.

✔ Sound natural.

Avoid sounding like you are reading your CV.

---

# Professional Story Structure

Every answer should follow this structure.

Present

↓

Experience

↓

Specialization

↓

Current Role

↓

Future Goal

Example:

"I'm currently a Senior Data Engineer based in Porto. I have more than fifteen years of experience working with data and analytics and over six years specializing in cloud data engineering on Google Cloud Platform..."

---

# Question 1

## Tell me about yourself.

---

## Why Interviewers Ask This

This is not an icebreaker.

The interviewer wants to evaluate:

- communication skills
- confidence
- seniority
- career progression
- technical profile

---

## Short Answer (30 seconds)

> Hi, I'm Juan.
>
> I'm a Senior Data Engineer with more than fifteen years of experience working with data and analytics, including over six years specializing in Google Cloud Platform.
>
> Throughout my career I've designed scalable data pipelines, ETL and ELT solutions, and cloud data platforms for large organizations.
>
> I recently relocated to Portugal and I'm looking for a new opportunity where I can continue building modern data platforms while contributing my experience to an international team.

---

## Medium Answer (1 minute)

> Hi, I'm Juan, a Senior Data Engineer originally from Chile and currently based in Porto, Portugal.
>
> I have more than fifteen years of experience working with data and analytics, and during the last six years I've specialized in cloud data engineering using Google Cloud Platform.
>
> Throughout my career I've worked across telecommunications, retail, banking, e-commerce and energy industries, building scalable data platforms, ETL pipelines and analytics solutions.
>
> In my current position at Falabella Group, I design centralized event-driven data pipelines using technologies such as BigQuery, Pub/Sub, Cloud Run, Dataform, Spark and Python.
>
> My work focuses on transforming large volumes of behavioral data into reliable business products that support analytics and decision-making.
>
> After moving to Portugal, I'm looking for my next challenge as a Senior Data Engineer within an international company where I can continue growing professionally.

---

## Long Answer (2–3 minutes)

Practice only after mastering the Medium Answer.

The goal is not to memorize a speech but to explain your professional journey naturally.

Structure:

- Current role
- Previous experience
- Technical specialization
- Biggest achievements
- Why Portugal
- Future goals

---

## Vocabulary

| Word | Meaning |
|-------|----------|
| scalable | able to grow efficiently |
| event-driven | architecture based on events |
| ingestion | data collection process |
| pipeline | automated data workflow |
| stakeholder | business or technical decision maker |
| deployment | releasing software into production |
| reliability | system stability |
| maintainability | ease of maintenance |
| architecture | system design |
| optimization | performance improvement |

---

## Useful Expressions

Instead of saying...

"I worked with BigQuery."

Say...

"I've been working extensively with BigQuery."

---

Instead of...

"I made ETLs."

Say...

"I designed and implemented scalable ETL and ELT pipelines."

---

Instead of...

"I know Python."

Say...

"Python has been one of my primary tools for building data engineering solutions."

---

Instead of...

"I have experience."

Say...

"I've had the opportunity to work on..."

# Follow-up Questions

These questions almost always follow.

- Tell me more about Falabella.
- What kind of data do you process?
- Why did you specialize in Data Engineering?
- Why are you interested in this position?
- What has been your biggest technical challenge?
- What project are you most proud of?

---

# Common Mistakes

❌ I work since six years...

✔ I've been working as a Data Engineer for more than six years.

---

❌ I made ETLs.

✔ I designed ETL pipelines.

---

❌ I know BigQuery.

✔ I have extensive experience working with BigQuery.

---

❌ I like challenges.

✔ I enjoy solving complex engineering problems.

---

# Practice Exercise

Repeat the Medium Answer five times.

Do not memorize every word.

Focus on communicating the ideas naturally.

After that:

Record yourself speaking for one minute.

Listen to the recording.

Ask yourself:

- Did I sound natural?
- Did I hesitate?
- Did I translate from Spanish?
- Which words were difficult?

Repeat until your explanation flows naturally.

# Question 2

## Why did you move to Portugal?

### Why Interviewers Ask This

The interviewer wants to know whether your relocation is stable and whether you intend to build your career in Europe.

---

### Model Answer

> My wife and I decided to move to Portugal because we wanted to build our long-term future in Europe.
>
> Portugal offered a great combination of quality of life, safety and professional opportunities, especially within the technology sector.
>
> Since moving here I've focused on improving my English, adapting to the European job market and preparing for my next professional challenge.

---

# Question 3

## Why are you looking for a new position?

### Model Answer

> I'm very grateful for everything I've learned in my current position.
>
> However, after relocating to Portugal, I'm looking for an opportunity that allows me to continue growing professionally while working closer to where I now live.
>
> I'm particularly interested in joining an international engineering team where I can contribute my experience building cloud data platforms while continuing to learn from experienced professionals.

---

# Question 4

## Why should we hire you?

### Model Answer

> I believe I offer a combination of strong technical expertise and business understanding.
>
> Throughout my career I've built scalable data platforms that have generated measurable business value across different industries.
>
> I enjoy solving complex technical problems, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams and continuously improving data platforms.
>
> I also adapt quickly to new technologies and environments, which has allowed me to successfully work across multiple industries throughout my career.

---

# Question 5

## What are your strengths?

### Model Answer

> I would highlight three strengths.
>
> First, I have extensive experience designing cloud data platforms at scale.
>
> Second, I always try to balance technical excellence with business objectives.
>
> Finally, I enjoy collaborating with both technical and non-technical stakeholders to deliver practical solutions.

10 minutes.

Goal:

---

# Success Criteria

You have completed this chapter when:

- You can introduce yourself naturally.
- You no longer read from notes.
- You can answer for 30 seconds, one minute or three minutes.
- You can explain your career without translating from Spanish.
- Your introduction sounds like a conversation rather than a presentation.
